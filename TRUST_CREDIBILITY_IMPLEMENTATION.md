## 3. Case Studies (`/case-studies`)

### Strategic Purpose:
Transform Mohishree from "vendor that cleans" into "partner that solves business problems."

### Implementation Details:

#### **4 Complete Case Studies**

**Case Study 1: Educational Institution**
- **Client:** Leading Educational Institution in Aurangabad
- **Challenge:** 2,000+ student campus, hygiene standards, scheduling flexibility
- **Solution:** Customized cleaning schedule, eco-friendly products, off-peak hours
- **Results:**
  - 15% reduction in student illness-related absenteeism
  - 98% faculty satisfaction rating
  - Multi-year contract renewal
  - 2+ years ongoing partnership

**Case Study 2: IT Park Corporate Complex**
- **Client:** IT Park with multiple companies
- **Challenge:** 24/7 operations, professional image, confidentiality
- **Solution:** Dual-shift teams, workstation sanitization, document handling protocols
- **Results:**
  - 50,000+ sq.ft managed across 5 buildings
  - 92% employee satisfaction rating
  - Zero security incidents
  - Contract expansion to 3 additional buildings

**Case Study 3: Premium Shopping Mall**
- **Client:** Shopping mall with 100+ retail tenants
- **Challenge:** 10,000+ daily visitors, continuous cleanliness, minimal disruption
- **Solution:** Continuous maintenance + post-closing deep cleaning, rapid response team
- **Results:**
  - 25% improvement in mystery shopper scores
  - 100% health inspection pass rate
  - Extended contract with facade cleaning addition

**Case Study 4: Pharmaceutical Manufacturing Unit**
- **Client:** Pharmaceutical manufacturer
- **Challenge:** Regulatory compliance, contamination control, documentation
- **Solution:** Certified personnel, validated procedures, approved agents, QA coordination
- **Results:**
  - Zero FDA cleaning-related observations
  - 30% reduction in production downtime
  - Preferred vendor status achieved
  - 3+ years continuous service

3. **The Mohishree Solution** - Strategy & implementation
4. **The Result** - Quantifiable/qualitative outcomes
