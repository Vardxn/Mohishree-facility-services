## 12. Blueprint Replication Guide

### For Creating New Service Pages:

1. **Copy Template Structure** from existing pages (Water Tank or Sofa Cleaning)

2. **Customize Sections:**
   - Update metadata (title, description, keywords)
   - Modify hero section (service-specific icon, headline, description)
   - Adapt process steps (4-8 steps depending on service complexity)
   - List relevant benefits (3-6 key advantages)
   - Define target audience

3. **Color Scheme Selection:**
   - Choose gradient that reflects service nature
   - Maintain consistent button/CTA colors
   - Use green for eco-friendly highlights

4. **Content Guidelines:**
   - Introduction: Problem + Solution + Mohishree's Approach (3 paragraphs)
   - Process: Transparent, numbered steps with descriptions
   - Benefits: Client-focused, outcome-oriented
   - CTA: Service-specific action prompts

5. **SEO Checklist:**
   - [ ] Location in title (Aurangabad)
   - [ ] Service keyword in H1
   - [ ] Meta description under 160 characters
   - [ ] Internal links to related services
   - [ ] Alt text for images (when added)
